About Us

A small bakery, built on family recipes

Brown Sugar Bakery started as a single oven in a home kitchen, baking cakes for neighbours and friends. What began as a hobby slowly turned into a small shop that still follows the same recipes — just with a few more ovens and a much bigger sweet tooth to satisfy.

We believe good baking does not need shortcuts. Our cakes are layered by hand, our ice creams are churned in small batches, and our chocolates are tempered the slow way. No preservatives, no overnight batters — just things made the morning you eat them.
